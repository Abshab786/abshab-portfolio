Protecting Your Data

1. Introduction

At AB SHAB, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (https://abshab.com).

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, Email Address, and any details provided in your contact message.
  • Technical Data: Device Information (model, OS), Browser Information (type, version), and IP address.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve user experience.

3. How We Use Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To reply to your inquiries and project requests.
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our website.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences (like dark mode), and analyze website traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly.

5. Third Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Analytics: For analyzing website traffic and user behavior.
  • Google Play Services: For Android applications published by AB SHAB.
  • Firebase: For app analytics, crash reporting, and backend services.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.
